1) „Setup” z beata (najprościej)

Wymagane tylko połączenie do Twojej Kibany/Elastica (internet niepotrzebny).
Na maszynie z beatem uruchom:

Filebeat

filebeat.exe setup --dashboards ^
  -E setup.kibana.host=https://<kibana:port> ^
  -E setup.kibana.username=<user> ^
  -E setup.kibana.password=<pass> ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.hosts=["https://<es:9200>"] ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.username=<user> ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.password=<pass>


Winlogbeat

winlogbeat.exe setup --dashboards ^
  -E setup.kibana.host=https://<kibana:port> ^
  -E setup.kibana.username=<user> ^
  -E setup.kibana.password=<pass> ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.hosts=["https://<es:9200>"] ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.username=<user> ^
  -E output.elasticsearch.password=<pass>
